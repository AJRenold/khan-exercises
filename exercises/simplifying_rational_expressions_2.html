\dfrac{NUMERATOR}{DENOMINATOR} = \dfrac{(FACTOR1)(X + A)}{(FACTOR2)(X + A)}

Notice that the term (X + A) appears in both the numerator and denominator.

Dividing both the numerator and denominator by (X + A) gives:
